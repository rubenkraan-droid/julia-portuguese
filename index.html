About me

Warm, patient, and all about real conversation

Your photo here

I'm a native Brazilian Portuguese speaker with a background in university tutoring, and I love helping learners speak confidently and naturally. My lessons focus on real-life conversation — we talk, laugh, and along the way I give gentle, natural corrections so you keep your flow.

Everyone learns differently, so I adapt to your pace and your goals, whether you're a complete beginner or polishing an accent you already have. The vibe is relaxed and easy-going, never stressful.
